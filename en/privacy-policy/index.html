Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. 100% Local and Private Processing

At Convertify, your data privacy is our utmost priority. Unlike other online services, all of our image processing tools (conversion, resizing, compression, and PDF generation) run 100% client-side.

This means that any images or documents you drop and process on this site are never uploaded to any remote server or transmitted over the internet. All processing occurs locally in your device's RAM and processor (computer, phone, or tablet) via your web browser.

2. Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, email addresses, or identity data. Since we do not require user account registration to use our tools, your navigation is entirely anonymous.

3. Local Storage (Cookies & Web Storage)

This site does not use tracking cookies or advertising pixels to track your online behavior. We only use standard browser storage technologies (Web Storage) for strictly functional purposes:

  • localStorage (Visual Theme): We save your theme preference (light or dark) to apply it automatically during your next visits.
  • sessionStorage (Language): We temporarily store that you have already been redirected to your appropriate language page to prevent redundant redirect loops during your active browser session.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website includes links to external and sponsored platforms (such as Buy Me a Coffee, Fiverr, or Hostinger). By clicking on these links, you will be redirected to their respective websites. We recommend reading the individual privacy policies of those platforms, as Convertify has no control over their terms of privacy or commercial practices.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our policies or the local operation of our tools, you can contact us through our official project support channel.
