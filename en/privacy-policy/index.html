Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

1. 100% Local and Private Processing

At My Local Picture, your data privacy is our utmost priority. Unlike other online services, all of our image processing tools (conversion, resizing, compression, and PDF generation) run 100% client-side.

This means that any images or documents you drop and process on this site are never uploaded to any remote server or transmitted over the internet. All processing occurs locally in your device's RAM and processor (computer, phone, or tablet) via your web browser.

2. Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, email addresses, or identity data. Since we do not require user account registration to use our tools, your navigation is entirely anonymous.

3. Cookies and Local Storage (Google Analytics & AdSense)

We use standard browser storage technologies (Web Storage) for functional purposes (such as saving your visual theme preference in localStorage and your language in sessionStorage).

Additionally, to keep this service free and analyze web performance, we integrate Google services that may place cookies on your browser:

  • Google AdSense: Google uses cookies (such as the DoubleClick/DART cookie) to serve ads based on users' prior visits to this or other websites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
  • Google Analytics: We use this tool to analyze site traffic and understand how users interact with our tools. Google collects anonymous browsing data for this purpose.

You can configure or decline the use of these third-party cookies at any time through our cookie consent banner or by clearing cookies in your browser.

4. Third-Party Links & Advertisements

Our website includes links to external and sponsored platforms (such as Buy Me a Coffee, Fiverr, or Hostinger), as well as ads served by Google AdSense. Clicking on these links or advertisements will redirect you to their respective third-party websites. We recommend reading the individual privacy policies of those platforms, as My Local Picture has no control over their terms of privacy or commercial practices.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our policies or the local operation of our tools, you can contact us through our official project support channel.
