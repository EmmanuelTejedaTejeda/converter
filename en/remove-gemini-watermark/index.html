Gemini Watermark Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Why do Gemini images have a watermark?

Google adds these marks for safety and transparency, helping viewers identify if an image was generated synthetically by an artificial intelligence, preventing misinformation.

How does this Gemini logo remover work?

The remover utilizes an inpainting algorithm based on the Laplace equation and inverse square distances. Instead of blurring, it reads pixels adjacent to the box and projects them inward, seamlessly recreating the background.

Can I remove the invisible watermark (SynthID)?

No. SynthID is Google's technology that embeds micro-modulations directly into the pixel structure. This invisible mark stays even after edits. Our tool removes the **visible corner logo**, which is enough to make your image look clean and professional.
