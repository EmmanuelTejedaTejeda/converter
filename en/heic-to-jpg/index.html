Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is HEIC and why should I convert it?

HEIC is the default format for photos on Apple devices since iOS 11. While efficient, it is not supported natively by Windows, Android, or many websites. Converting to JPG ensures universal compatibility.

Is this conversion safe and private?

Yes. Your photos are processed entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. No files are uploaded to any servers.

Can I convert HEIC to PNG as well?

Yes, you can select PNG from the output formats list if you need lossless quality or transparency support.

Are there any limits or registration requirements?

No, it is completely free, does not require an account, and has no file size or count limitations.
