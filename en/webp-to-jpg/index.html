Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Why convert WebP to JPG?

WebP is a great modern web format, but it is not universally supported. Many online forms, legacy editors, government websites, and school portals still reject WebP. JPG ensures maximum compatibility everywhere.

Is this conversion tool free?

Yes. You can convert all your WebP images to JPG without paying anything and without creating an account. There are no size caps or daily file limits.

Why is my privacy guaranteed?

Unlike online cloud services, your images are never sent to external servers. The conversion happens entirely locally inside your browser cache. Nobody else can see or access your files.

How is transparency handled when WebP is converted to JPG?

Since JPG is an opaque image format and doesn't support a transparent alpha channel, any transparent sections in your WebP image will be automatically filled with a solid white background.
