Complete Guide: How to Split Midjourney 2x2 Grid Images

AI image generators, particularly Midjourney, output results in a 2x2 collage of 4 distinct images. When you want to save, upload, or edit one of these panels on its own, you normally have to manually crop them in an external image editor. Our online utility automates this, segmenting the grid into 4 standalone images with a single click in a matter of seconds.

Benefits of Using a Client-Side Midjourney Splitter

  • Time-saving: Avoid opening Photoshop, Paint, or any other editing suite to crop each quadrant manually.
  • Pixel-perfect sharpness: Canvas-based splitting maintains the original mathematical rendering without losing detail.
  • ZIP Download: Download the split images individually or together in a clean, unified ZIP archive.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - Midjourney Splitter

Is this tool secure for private images?

Yes. The entire splitting and zipping process runs on the client-side within your browser. None of your files are sent to the internet or stored on our servers.

Does it work with other AI generators?

Yes. While optimized for Midjourney grids, it works with any image you want to split into 4 equal quadrants, including older DALL-E grid styles or customized grids from Stable Diffusion.

How many images can I process at once?

The free version allows batch processing of up to 5 grids at a time (producing 20 individual output images). For unlimited batch size and professional features, you can upgrade to PRO for just $2/month.
