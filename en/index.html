Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) about Convertify

What is Convertify?

Convertify is a comprehensive suite of free online image processing tools that allows you to convert, compress, resize, and edit your photos directly from your browser without installing any software.

How does local image processing work?

Unlike other websites that upload your files to cloud servers, Convertify processes all your images 100% locally in your device's memory and processor. Your photos never leave your computer or mobile phone, ensuring unmatched security and speed.

Is it safe to use Convertify for my private photos?

Yes, it is completely safe and private. Since all operations occur client-side (in your browser) and we do not store or transmit your files over the internet, no one else has access to your images at any time.

What image file formats are supported?

We support conversion and optimization among a wide range of popular formats, including JPG, PNG, WebP, SVG, Apple's HEIC, and even PDF documents, all with multiple custom quality and size options.
