Complete Guide: Erasing DALL-E and Copilot AI Watermarks

Images generated by models such as DALL-E 3 (via ChatGPT Plus or Microsoft Copilot / Bing Image Creator) often contain a small colored watermark in the bottom-left corner. If you plan to use these images in professional slideshows, web pages, or social media posts, these logos can be highly distracting. Our free web tool helps you clean your art without blur side effects.

How does the Laplace local inpainting work?

Instead of executing file uploads to a backend server, our algorithm executes client-side. When clicking 'Remove Watermark', it executes a Jacobi Laplace diffusion algorithm that diffuses the colors from the box boundary into the mask. The result is a smooth, reconstructed background.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - DALL-E Watermarks

Can I remove watermarks from other AI tools?

Yes. Although we provide quick preset buttons for DALL-E 3 (bottom-left) and Gemini (bottom-right), you can manually drag, position, and resize the box over any logo or text you want to erase.

Will my AI image lose quality?

No. Only the pixels within the selected box are re-evaluated. The rest of the image (more than 99% of your artwork) remains completely unaltered and sharp.

Are there any daily limits?

No. The tool is free and unlimited. You can process as many images as you need, without signing up or paying fees.
